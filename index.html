About Me!

My name is Frank Timmons. I've lived in Portland, OR for my whole life. I graduated from Grant High School, and decided to start working right away. My first job after high school was in the mailroom at the Wieden+Kennedy ad agency, I had a temp position during the holidays because there were more packages around that time of year and they needed some extra help. After that I got a job at a 3D printing and scuplture foundry called Form 3D. My main role there was being the head of the shipping department, but I also ended up learning a lot about the computer programs that go into 3D printing, and some more hands on artistic stuff. I worked there for 3 years but I was feeling like I needed a change due to lack of organization, and the fear of being around toxic chemicals all day long.

That is what has lead me here today to start my journey through Epicodus! I'm realy excited to continue through the course and learn more about the world of programming. My ultimate goal would be to become a game developer, but for now I'm just hoping to get any job that will strengthen my programming, problem solving skills, and teamwork so I can make games as a hobby.
